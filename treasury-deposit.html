Request Deposit from Governor into treasury wallet

Governance contract collects a portion of the inflation, as well as penalties from premature withdrawals. The OINK holdings are used to trigger events, burn tokens and pay bonuses for extending miners.
With this process the tokens can be transferred from the governing contract into the treasury wallet for storage(creating OINK reserves) and management(further withdrawals, payments,...)
